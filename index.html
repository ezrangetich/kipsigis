The Battle of Mogori – Circa 1889

Compiled By kiprono arap korgoren

Overview

The Battle of Mogori was a devastating military encounter between the Kipsigis on one hand and the Kisii and Luo on the other...

Timeline of the Battle

Early 1889 - Prophecy Ignored

The Kipsigis warriors, despite warnings from the Orkoiyot, prepared to raid Gusii land, assembling at Bureti.

Mid-1889 - Raid on Gusii

The warriors attacked Mugirango and Kitutu, destroying villages, but ignored the bad omens.

Late 1889 - Gusii Trap Set

After the Kipsigis warriors crossed into Luo land, the Gusii prepared an ambush along Manga Escarpment.

Late 1889 - Battle Begins

The Kipsigis, retreating from the Luo, were ambushed by the Gusii at Getwanyi in Kitutu, leading to a devastating loss. The warriors, unprepared for the trap, found themselves surrounded, with no easy escape. The battle was fierce, lasting for hours, as the Kipsigis fought to break through the encirclement. The numerical and strategic advantage of the Gusii and Luo proved overwhelming.

1890 - Aftermath & Consequences

The Kipsigis suffered great losses, leading to premature initiation of boys into warriors and early marriages. The defeat also changed their military strategies in future conflicts, making them more cautious in raids.
